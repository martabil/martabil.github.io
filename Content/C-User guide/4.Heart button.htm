    Heart button

    Figure 17: Duolingo: heart button

    Doulingo introduced gaming like marking and heart button indicates how many lives you have left. You loose a heart every time you make a mistake and your 5 hearts are replenished each day. They come every 5 hours and you can check how much time is left to receive a new one, as in the picture below showing 12'48'' for a new heart to appear.

    Figure18: Duolingo: time left to get new heart

    If you run out of hearts you can choose to practice and you'll receive additional heart on completion of every practice round. One of the benefits of this heart practice session is that you don't actually loose heart for making a mistake.

     

    Figure 19: Duolingo:a way to get heart with practice

    You can refill your hearts with gems (blue icon below the hearts). It is a useful option of you run out of hearts but you still want to continue practice.

    Figure 20: a way to get hearts refilled for gems
