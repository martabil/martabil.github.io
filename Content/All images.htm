What is Doulingo? Duolingo is the most popular language-learning platform and the most downloaded education app in the world, with more than 300 million users. The company's mission is to make education free, fun, and accessible to all. Duolingo is designed to feel like a game and scientifically proven to be effective.Duolingo offers a skill tree of lessons that use listening exercises, flashcards, and multiple choice questions to drill you on new words, phrases, and sentences.The service also has community features that let you connect with other people who are learning the same language you are. What purpose Doulingo can help you achieve?If you are a beginner and never have had contact with language before and want to to start an adventure with a new foregin language or whether you already have had some knowledge, for example you studied Spanish at high school and want refresh it, then Duolingo is perfect for you. Why Doulingo is for you ?If you don't like learning from books becasue it is simply too boring for you, if you would like to learn in enjoyable and active way then do not hesitate to download Doulingo. Thanks to colorful layout and funny characters that will lead you through exercises you will see that learning a language can be fun. Moreover, exercises are cratead in such a way that grammar, vocabulary, speaking and listening is one place. Each module offers different subject in order to develop all necessarry language skills. How Duolingo can help me?Doulingo, in easy way, will help you to expand your vocabulary, learn grammar, practise speaking and listening.       Doulingo is free but from time to time there might appear an ad. Just tap to close the ad and continue your educational journey. Figure 2: Example of an advertisement in Duolingo

Heart buttonFigure 17: Duolingo: heart buttonDoulingo introduced gaming like marking and heart button indicates how many lives you have left. You loose a heart every time you make a mistake and your 5 hearts are replenished each day. They come every 5 hours and you can check how much time is left to receive a new one, as in the picture below showing 12'48'' for a new heart to appear. Figure18: Duolingo: time left to get new heartIf you run out of hearts you can choose to practice and you'll receive additional heart on completion of every practice round. One of the benefits of this heart practice session is that you don't actually loose heart for making a mistake.  Figure 19: Duolingo:a way to get heart with practice You can refill your hearts with gems (blue icon below the hearts). It is a useful option of you run out of hearts but you still want to continue practice. Figure 20: a way to get hearts refilled for gems

Exercises buttonFigure 20: Duolingo: User guide - Exercises buttonIn the bottom left hand corner is exercises button. You can see where you are working in Doulingo at the moment. Click Exercises button and it will take you to the main menu tree structure. Number in the crown next to the circle signifies completed level, as per below in Activities and Preferences only one level was completed, for Routines no levels are visible as not started by the user, Household and following circles are grayed out . The rule in Duolingo is that you have to have at least level 1 of two proceeding lessons in order to open next two lessons. Figure 22: How next lessons are uncovered

StoriesFigure 32: Duolingo: Stories optionOnce you get part way through the tree and you start feeling more confident with the language use feature called Stories. Stories are unlocked once you complete 10 levels or receive 10 crowns. Stories are conversations where you are asked questions regarding the vocabulary used in the conversation.  Figure 33: What stories screen look likeYou can stop the recording at any moment and repeat the phrases after the speaker. It is especially helpful for beginners to get used to natural rhythm and intonation of a foreign language.
