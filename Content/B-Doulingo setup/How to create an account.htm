    Doulingo setup

    This short user guide shows you how to download the application and create an account.

    1. Go to Play Store (Android)/Apple Store (ios) and search for Doulingo. When you find it click Download / Zainstaluj button.

     

     

     

     

     

     

    Figure 3: Duolingo download

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

    2. If you are a new user and don't have an account click on Get started

    Figure 4: Doulingo set up: Get started

    3. On next screen choose where you heard about Duolingo, you have following options to choose from:

    • Online ads

    • Web search

    • Social media

    • Radio

    • Friends and family

    • TV

    • App store

    • Other

    Figure 5: Dulingo set up: How did you hear about us question.

    4. Choose the reason why you are learning a language, you have following options to choose from:

    • School

    • Culture

    • Travel

    • Job opportunities

    • Family & Friends

    • Brain training

    • Other

    Figure 6: Duolingo setup: Why are you learning a language question.

     

    5. On next screen showing course overview click Continue

    Figure 7: Doulingo setup: Continue button

    6. Pick a daily goal that you would like to achieve every day, choose from:

    • Casual - 5 minutes a day

    • Regular - 10 minutes a day

    • Serious - 15 minutes a day

    • Insane - 20 minutes a day

    Figure 8: Duolino setup: Pick a goal

    7. Choose your level

    • Learning Spanish for the first time?

    • Already know some Spanish?

    Figure 9: Duolingo setup: Choose your level

    After this final setup step you can enjoy learning with Doulingo!
