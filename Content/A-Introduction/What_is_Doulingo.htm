    What is Doulingo?

     

    Duolingo is the most popular language-learning platform and the most downloaded education app in the world, with more than 300 million users. The company's mission is to make education free, fun, and accessible to all. Duolingo is designed to feel like a game and scientifically proven to be effective.

    Duolingo offers a skill tree of lessons that use listening exercises, flashcards, and multiple choice questions to drill you on new words, phrases, and sentences.

    The service also has community features that let you connect with other people who are learning the same language you are.

     

    What purpose Doulingo can help you achieve?

    If you are a beginner and never have had contact with language before and want to to start an adventure with a new foregin language or whether you already have had some knowledge, for example you studied Spanish at high school and want refresh it, then Duolingo is perfect for you.

    Why Doulingo is for you ?

    If you don't like learning from books becasue it is simply too boring for you, if you would like to learn in enjoyable and active way then do not hesitate to download Doulingo. Thanks to colorful layout and funny characters that will lead you through exercises you will see that learning a language can be fun. Moreover, exercises are cratead in such a way that grammar, vocabulary, speaking and listening is one place. Each module offers different subject in order to develop all necessarry language skills.

    How Duolingo can help me?

    Doulingo, in easy way, will help you to expand your vocabulary, learn grammar, practise speaking and listening.
