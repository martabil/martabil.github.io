Frequently Asked Questions

How much do Duolingo cost?

You can learn languages on Duolingo completely free. You can use it on your computer and sync it with our free apps for your mobile device.

Do I need the internet to use Duolingo?

Yes! If you are accessing Duolingo via the web or the Android and iOS apps, you will need to be connected to the internet.

If you have a weak internet connection, you may see delays with lessons loading or lost streaks due to syncing issues. Be sure to check your connection strength if you encounter loading and syncing issues.

How can I use Duolingo?

You can learn with Duolingo via our iOS app, Android app, and web (mobile or desktop)..
